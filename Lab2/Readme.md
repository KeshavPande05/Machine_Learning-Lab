### 1. Covariance Matrix

For a design or feature matrix:

$$
X = \begin{bmatrix}
1 & 0 & 2 \\
0 & 1 & 1 \\
2 & 1 & 0 \\
1 & 1 & 1 \\
0 & 2 & 1
\end{bmatrix}
$$

Compute the covariance matrix using matrix multiplications. Verify your results by using numpy library operations.
